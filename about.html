About Our School

Vision, Mission & Goals

A. VISION:

"Smart, Religious, Competitive, Disciplined, Creative, Accomplished, and Indonesian Character."

B. Mission:

1. Organizing quality, active, creative, highly disciplined education, based on faith and piety and adhering to the nation's religious and cultural norms.

2. Carry out education and training with quality insight, professional, nationality, character, entrepreneurship.

3. Prepare graduates who excel in both academic and non-academic fields.

C.Goals:

1. Preparing students who believe and have faith, become productive human beings, responsible for developing professional attitudes in their expertise competencies.

2. Equipping students with science, technology and art, noble akhlaq, in petitioning in the Business World and the Industrial World as a middle-level workforce.

3. Produce graduates who are qualified, have national and entrepreneurial character, have environmental culture and fill the world of work
